Why Choose Us

Fully Outfitted
Adventures

Hawaii Forest & Trail prides itself on offering authentic experiences that allow visitors to connect deeply with Hawaii's natural beauty and cultural heritage. These experiences are designed to provide genuine insights into the island's ecosystems and traditions.

Expert
Guides

The company's team of expert guides are passionate about sharing their knowledge and love for Hawaii's landscapes, wildlife, and history. They are trained to provide enriching experiences, ensuring that every tour is both educational and enjoyable.

Small Group
Tours

With a focus on sustainability and personalized experiences, Hawaii Forest & Trail keeps its tour groups small, allowing for intimate interactions with nature and personalized attention from guides. This ensures that guests have a more immersive and meaningful experience.

Exclusive
Access

The company offers off-the-beaten-path adventures that take guests to lesser-known destinations and hidden gems across the Hawaiian Islands. These unique experiences provide an opportunity to explore untouched wilderness and discover secret spots that few tourists ever see.

Embark on an unforgettable journey with Hawaii Forest & Trail!

An island of fire and ice that reminds us nothing is permanent and there is always time to slow down and take in the beauty.

The Island of Hawaii offers endless adventures a perfect fit for travelers who crave the authentic and dare to seek out the tallest mountains in the world, volcanic craters, hidden lava tubes, black sand beaches, and natures tropical waterfalls. Hawaii Forest & Trail has been the premier Big Island adventure tour service for more than 25 years.

What continues to bring travelers back to Hawaii Forest & Trail is our team of highly knowledgeable Interpretive Guides, our unparalleled access to private land, and our intimate small group tour sizes. All that, paired with all the comforts youd expect from a first-class tour company, our experiences are designed to keep you comfortable and captivated.

We invite you to be our guest and experience the best spots in Hawaii Nei!
